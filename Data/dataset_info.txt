This folder references our data storage. Due to GitHub file size limits, our primary dataset is sourced directly from the official NYC Open Data portal for Motor Vehicle Collisions
Note for grading: 
Our filtered 2020 NYC collision dataset contains over 66,000 rows, making it too large for GitHub to preview directly in your browser. 
To view or grade our raw data layout, please click the "Download" or "View raw" button on the data files to open them directly in Excel or Power BI.
